About Us

Bhimai Construction & cleaning services

Bhimai Construction & Cleaning Services, led by Mr. Deepak Suryawanshi, is a premier general contracting and cleaning company based in Naregaon, Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad), Maharashtra. With roots going back to 1999, we have built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality civil construction, plumbing, and cleaning solutions to homes, businesses, and industrial clients across the region. Our skilled workforce, advanced machinery, and commitment to on-time project delivery make us the go-to choice for all construction needs.

At Bhimai Construction & Cleaning Services, we believe every project deserves precision, dedication, and innovative thinking. From structural building works and plumbing installations to professional cleaning services, we handle every job with the same passion and professionalism. Rated 4.8 out of 5 by our valued clients and available around the clock, we are always ready to serve you. We are proud to be a trusted name in Aurangabad and continue to grow as one of Maharashtra's leading construction companies.
